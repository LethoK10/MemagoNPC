About Memago NPC

Empowering Communities Through Practical Skills & Sustainable Development

Who We Are

Memago NPC is a nonprofit organization dedicated to uplifting communities through hands-on skills and sustainable solutions. We provide training in farming, plumbing, and building/construction, empowering individuals to create opportunities for themselves and their communities.

We partner with schools and local organizations to install water storage systems, improve sanitation, and deliver practical construction projects. Our goal is to ensure that communities have access to clean water, safe infrastructure, and essential skills for long-term self-reliance.

How We Uplift Communities

Our programs are designed to create tangible impact:

  • Farming Training: Teaching modern and sustainable agricultural practices to improve food security and generate income.
  • Plumbing Skills: Training individuals to maintain water systems and sanitation facilities, creating employment opportunities.
  • Building & Construction: Offering practical construction knowledge for safe housing, community infrastructure, and local development projects.
  • School Water Projects: Installing water storage systems to ensure children have consistent access to clean water.
  • Community Engagement: Workshops, youth training, and mentorship programs to promote self-reliance and leadership.
